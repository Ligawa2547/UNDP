## 📘 Overview

The **United Nations Online Education & Development Platform (UNOEDP)** is a digital infrastructure designed to support global education initiatives, institutional collaboration, and knowledge distribution aligned with United Nations development priorities.

The platform provides a secure, scalable environment for delivering educational programmes, managing institutional access, and supporting international capacity-building initiatives.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Purpose

UNOEDP is built to:

- 🌐 Expand access to digital education globally  
- 🎓 Support structured learning programmes and training initiatives  
- 🤝 Enable institutional collaboration and knowledge exchange  
- 🔐 Provide secure and compliant digital infrastructure  
- 📊 Support scalable deployment for multi-region programmes  
- ♿ Align with international accessibility and inclusion standards
